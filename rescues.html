Our Rescues

Here are just a few of the animals that Ontario Wildlife Rescue and it's generous volunteers have helped!

  • Robby Raccoon

    Robby was found on the side of the road, injured and tired. Thanks to a OWR volunteer, Robby quickly got the medical aid he needed and within months was rehabilitated and released back into the wild.

  • Yogi

    Yogi was found rummaging through the garbage on a busy street, severely malnourished. Due to donations and our hardworking volunteers, Yogi was able to be rehabilitated into the wild.

  • Pepe

    Pepe was found as a pup, lost and alone. OWR came just in time to help the lost little pup. He was succesfully integrated into another litter and now has been rerealesed with his new family.

  • Hedwig

    Hedwig was found with a sprained wing, but with time and help from our volunteers Hedwig was able to be rereleased and has been flying high ever since!

  • Bucky

    Bucky was found far down river from the nearest beaver dam. OWR tended to his wounds and rereleased Bucky back into the wild to get back to work!
